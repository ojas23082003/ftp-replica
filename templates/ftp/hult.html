About Hult Prize

Hult Prize is an international competition that collates ideas from students by challenging them to solve a pressing social issue revolving around topics such as food security, water access, energy, environment, and education. Hult Prize inspires student entrepreneurs to solve the world’s biggest challenges through innovative social enterprises with positive global impact. Joining Hult Prize will allow you to learn the necessary fundamentals to become a successful startup founder, Join a diverse global network of entrepreneurs, staff and experts and most importantly help you achieve both, personal and professional growth.

International Relations Cell

The International Relations Cell (IRC) is a student body working along with the Office of International Relations (OIR). It works to promote the exchange of students, faculties, and ideas between IIT Kharagpur and her international partners and establishing the globalization of IIT Kharagpur.
