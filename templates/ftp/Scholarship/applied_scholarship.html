Scholarship Details

Scholarship: Guru Krupa IITKGP Foundation Scholarship

Funding Agency: IIT Kharagpur Foundation (IITKGF)

Deadline: 20th April 2022, 11:59 PM

Details:

Guru Kripa Foundation offers financial support to enable five of the top students from IITKGP, of which at least one is from ECE department and four from any other department, school or center to avail high quality international research and professional internships (expected to last 8-12 weeks, most likely during the summer holidays). The foundation has also offered to provide financial support to five incoming international students from top universities across the world for short term research or academic stays for a period of 2 months upto a semester.

This program aims at creating summer student fellowships in IITKGP that will fund international summer internships for students at well recognised universities outside India.
