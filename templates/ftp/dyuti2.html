Dyuti

The Indian Institute of Technology at Kharagpur is on of the most coveted higher education institutes in India. It was established with a novel mission of providing broad-based education, motivation and training of its students to lead the society as the stalwarts of their respective fields. In furtherance of this motto, we are glad to invite international students from all over India for ”Dyuti”, meaning Light, a 5-day Techno-Cultural workshop, tentatively from 23rd-27th October to illuminate the lives of its participants with a plethora of Indian activities and indigenous Deepawali paraphernalia. All participants will receive University credits and a certificate of completion in addition to the experience of unique Indian culture and how Diwali works at IIT Kharagpur! Tentative cost for the workshop including accommodation is INR 6000-8000.
