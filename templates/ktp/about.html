About Knowledge Transfer Portal

Knowledge Transfer Portal (KTP) is a part of the International Relations Cell under the Office of International Relations (OIR) that aims to streamline the knowledge which ensures that everyone on the team has the information they need to keep the cell functioning smoothly. Knowledge transfer is important as it improves innovation, collaboration, and understanding among the team members. Rather than relying on facts and data to share information across the tenures, the portal is better able to paint a holistic picture of complicated concepts.
